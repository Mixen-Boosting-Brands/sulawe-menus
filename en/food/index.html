Drink Responsibly

All our prices include taxes in Mexican national currency.

If you have any allergies, please notify our staff.

Consuming raw or undercooked meats, poultry, seafood, eggs may increase your risk of foodborne illnesses.

The weight of our dishes is measured raw.

Prices are subject to change without prior notice.

Certain dishes are subject of availability because they are products from other States of Mexico.
